Common Issues

Can't log in?

Check your email and password. Use "Forgot Password" to reset.

File upload issues?

Ensure file is under 50MB and in an allowed format (PDF, DOCX, XLSX, JPG, PNG).

Missing grades?

Grades may be pending approval. Contact your teacher or admin for status.
