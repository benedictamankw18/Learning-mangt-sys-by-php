Cookie Policy

Last Updated: February 15, 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our Learning Management System platform. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our platform.

Cookies are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners. They enable certain functionality and help us understand which parts of our platform are most useful to users.

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Essential Cookies

Purpose: Required for the platform to function properly

Duration: Session or persistent

Can be disabled: No - Required for platform operation

These cookies are necessary for:

  • Authentication and login functionality
  • Security features and fraud prevention
  • Remembering your session as you navigate pages
  • Enabling core platform features

Examples:

  • lms_session - Maintains your login session
  • lms_csrf_token - Protects against cross-site request forgery attacks
  • lms_auth_token - Secures your authentication

2. Functional Cookies

Purpose: Remember your preferences and settings

Duration: Typically 1 year

Can be disabled: Yes - But may affect user experience

These cookies enable:

  • Language and region preferences
  • User interface customizations
  • Dashboard layout preferences
  • Notification settings
  • Accessibility options

Examples:

  • lms_language - Stores your preferred language
  • lms_theme - Remembers dark/light mode preference
  • lms_dashboard_layout - Saves your dashboard configuration

3. Analytics Cookies

Purpose: Help us understand platform usage and improve user experience

Duration: Typically 2 years

Can be disabled: Yes

These cookies collect anonymous information about:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • How users navigate through the platform
  • Error messages encountered
  • Platform performance metrics
  • General demographic information (region, device type)

Examples:

  • _ga - Google Analytics identifier
  • _gid - Google Analytics session identifier
  • lms_analytics - Our internal analytics tracking

4. Performance Cookies

Purpose: Improve platform speed and performance

Duration: Session or up to 1 year

Can be disabled: Yes - But may slow down the platform

These cookies help us:

  • Load pages faster through caching
  • Optimize content delivery
  • Balance server load
  • Monitor platform stability

Cookie Duration

Cookies can be categorized by how long they last:

Session Cookies

These temporary cookies are deleted when you close your browser. They help us maintain your session while you're actively using the platform.

Persistent Cookies

These cookies remain on your device for a set period (ranging from days to years) and are activated each time you visit our platform. They help us remember your preferences and provide a personalized experience.

Third-Party Cookies

In addition to our own cookies, we may use third-party cookies from trusted partners to help us analyze platform usage and improve our services:

  • Google Analytics: Website analytics to understand user behavior
  • Content Delivery Networks (CDNs): To deliver content faster and more reliably
  • Video Platform Providers: For embedded educational videos

These third parties have their own privacy policies and cookie policies which govern how they use your information.

Managing Cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can exercise your cookie rights by setting your preferences in your browser settings.

Browser Settings

Most browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your settings to:

  • Block all cookies
  • Accept only first-party cookies
  • Delete cookies when you close your browser
  • Receive a warning before a cookie is stored

Browser-Specific Instructions

Google Chrome

Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data

Mozilla Firefox

Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data

Safari

Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data

Microsoft Edge

Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Cookies

Important: Blocking or deleting cookies may impact your ability to use certain features of our platform, including login functionality and personalized settings.

Your Cookie Consent

When you first visit our platform, you'll see a cookie banner asking for your consent to use non-essential cookies. You can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer of our platform or by accessing your account settings.

Other Tracking Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may use other tracking technologies:

Web Beacons (Pixels)

Small graphic images embedded in web pages and emails that help us understand user engagement and email open rates.

Local Storage

Browser storage that allows us to save data locally on your device for faster loading times and offline functionality.

Device Fingerprinting

Collection of device information (browser type, screen resolution, etc.) for security and fraud prevention purposes.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our business practices. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
